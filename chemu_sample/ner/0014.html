Synthesis of compound 50-e
A mixture of compound 50-f and water (40mL) was heated to reflux for 2hrs, cooled to room temperature, white solid was precipitated and filtered out. Solid was washed with water (20mL×3), dried under vacuum to give compound 50-e (1.6g, yield 64%). The product was used directly for the next step without further purification.
